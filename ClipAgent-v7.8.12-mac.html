ClipAgent release notes

Version 7.8.12

  • Creators now show their live recording state directly on the creator card, including active job status, finalizing state, and the latest saved output.
  • Added a direct Stop recording action on creator cards so users no longer need to jump to the Jobs tab to stop a Twitch live capture.
  • Clarified stop/finalization messaging so jobs visibly move through Recording, Stopping, Finalizing, and Stopped states.
